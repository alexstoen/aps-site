## Selection Philosophy

After reviewing every image in the catalog, selections were made on a single criterion: **does this image make a commercial client trust and hire the business within 3 seconds?**

The portfolio spans Colorado — not just Colorado Springs city limits — because the strongest images are the strongest images. The homepage hero and service cards are anchored to CS identity. The portfolio demonstrates geographic range and technical capability across the state.

## Future Shoot Recommendations (Priority Order)

1. **Twilight golden-hour residential with interior lights** — Schedule dedicated evening session on a luxury listing (Broadmoor area, Flying Horse, or Black Forest). The Jan 2026 twilight shot demonstrates the value; replicate with summer evening light.

2. **Alex in frame with equipment** — One well-composed image of Alex at a job site, hi-vis vest, drone visible, CS skyline or mountains in background. This is the single biggest trust gap in the entire catalog. No human presence in 166 images.

3. **Construction sequence (3-shot series)** — Land one construction monitoring client and document 3 flights across 60+ days. The before/during/after sequence is the most compelling construction sales tool that doesn't exist yet.

4. **Annotated roof inspection deliverable** — One real inspection image with callout overlays showing identified damage. Shows the actual product, not just the photography.

5. **Dedicated Garden of the Gods oblique** — The April 2024 shot is strong but taken from high altitude facing the mountains. A lower, tighter oblique of the formations with CS visible behind would be a more commercially compelling composition.

6. **Fall foliage residential listing** — A single-family home in the foothills surrounded by fall aspen color. This is the highest-converting real estate aerial in Colorado.
