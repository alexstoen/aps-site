About

Precision and

Aerial Professional Services is a veteran-owned drone services company based in Colorado Springs, Colorado. Founded and operated by a U.S. Navy veteran and FAA Part 107 certified remote pilot.

Every project — from a single residential listing to an ongoing construction site — is approached with the same standard: plan the mission, execute it safely, and deliver work you can use immediately.

We serve real estate agents, roofing contractors, construction companies, property managers, and local businesses throughout El Paso County and surrounding areas.
